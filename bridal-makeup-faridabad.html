HD Makeup vs Airbrush Makeup — What Should You Choose?

Many brides are confused between HD makeup and airbrush makeup. Here’s a simple breakdown:

  • HD Makeup: Gives a natural skin-like finish, ideal for most brides
  • Airbrush Makeup: Lightweight spray finish, perfect for oily skin and long events

We recommend the best option based on your skin type, weather, and event duration.
